inlinestatic

Convert string to a bool.

Valid bool values are yes, no, true, false and simple digit numbers. Case independent. All numbers other than zero are returned as YES, however, the check is quick, so a leading zero will always be a NO, whereas a leading digit will always be a YES (e.g. 0123 will be NO)
