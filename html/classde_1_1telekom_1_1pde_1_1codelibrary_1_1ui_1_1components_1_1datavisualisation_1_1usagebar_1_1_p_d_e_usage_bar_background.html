Detailed Description

Background View of PDEUsageBar.

This is just a simple view that shows two rectangles next to each other, one filled with the primary (fill) color and one with the secondary (empty) color. They share the same space, so when the width of the left rectangle grows, the width of the right rectangle shrinks. This is an easy way to visualize a fill bar.
