Set the (animatable) title color.

If no title color is given, the title color is calculated from a) the main color, and b) the hints (indicative buttons have a special color), and c) the system preset (dark or light system).

The shadow color is also calculated; the shadow color depends solely on the main color (and does only change inbetween states)
