Detailed Description

Basic button, controlled by agent states.

The button does nothing itself, it just manages sublayers which do the actual animation. Buttons can (but must not be) separated into background, foreground and overlay layers.

PDEButton offers a few predefined button variants as preset, but does not prevent you using your own graphics.
